Definition: Breakthroughs in Engagement, the Arts and Research (BEAR) Day and Engineering Expo Day showcase outstanding undergraduate student research and special projects. BEAR Day includes oral presentations, poster sessions, musical presentations and art show exhibits. The program this year has over 100 presentations with contributions from over 150 students. At the same time, Engineering Expo Day events feature oral and poster presentations on research and design projects and the annual Freshman Design Competition in the University Center arena, with over 300 students participating and presenting in the Engineering Expo program events. BEAR Day demonstrates and honors the achievements of students and highlights the depth and breadth of the academic experiences at Mercer.

History: Origianlly starting in 2010, the event showcases outstanding student research and special projects from the College of Liberal Arts, Stetson School of Business and Economics, Townsend School of Music and the School of Engineering. This will be the 6th annual Bear Day as well as the 15th annual Engineering Expo.
