About Me

Something about myself I would consider interesting would be the time I went sky diving for the first time. I was visiting my cousin in-law in Delaware and we decided to do something crazy and went sky diving. The flight prepping for the day went well and we got in the air just fine. The routine check of the parachutes was done as well but when we tried to use it it didn't work, I thought I was going to die but the company always packs a second safety chute and we used that at the last minute. Really freaky moment though.

Another interesting about me would be another incident that happened when I was much younger. I was around 2 years old and we had just came here from India, living in an apartment. My mom had gone to take a shower and left me watching tv, now I was a very mischievous baby so I was prone to doing this. I got a key from the coffee table and stuck it inside of an electrical outlet across the room, the outlet short-circuited and basically blew the wall up while I was standing there crying.
